Race To The Future

In a team of three, we've participated in Race to the future. The goal of the competition is to make an RC car self-driving and then compete for the fastest time on a track. The car knows where to go by detecting the edges of the track, using OpenCV's edgedetection. Next year we'd like to fine tune the AI that did not prove to work sufficiently enough for this year's entry.
